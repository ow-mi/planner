📅 Week Format Guide

Use YYYY-Www.f format where:

  • YYYY = 4-digit year (e.g., 2027)
  • ww = 2-digit ISO week (01-53)
  • f = day of week (1=Mon, 7=Sun)

Example: 2027-W18.6 = Friday of week 18, 2027
